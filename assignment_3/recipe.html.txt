        <h1>Apple Pie Recipe</h1>
        <p>This delicious recipe will serve 6 people.</p>
      </header>
      <section>
        <h3>Ingredients</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>2 9-inch pie crusts (one for the top crust, one for the bottom crust)</li>
          <li>1/4 cup all-purpose flour</li>
          <li>3/4 cup sugar</li>
          <li>1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon</li>
          <li>1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg</li>
          <li>Dash of salt</li>
          <li>2 tablespoons butter</li>
          <li>6 cups thinly sliced apples</li>
        </ul>
      </section>
      <section>
        <h3>Directions</h3>
        <ol>
          <li>Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.</li>
          <li>Mix sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour, and salt. Stir in the apples.</li>
          <li>Place into pie crust, dot with butter. Cover with top crust, and slit evenly to let steam escape. Seal the top crust to the bottom by pinching the edges together.</li>
          <li>Cover edge of the crust with a three inch strip of aluminum foil.</li>
          <li>Bake 40-50 minutes, until crust is brown and juice begins to bubble through top.</li>
        </ol>
